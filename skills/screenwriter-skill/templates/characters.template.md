## CHECK: ARE THE VOICES DISTINCT?

After filling in everyone — run the test:
1. Take a line from any character without the name attached.
2. Show it to someone else (or to yourself a day later).
3. It should be obvious who is speaking.

If two characters' lines sound the same — you don't have two characters, you have one. Differentiate the voices.
