## RULES OF EXPOSITION

When introducing the world to the audience — NEVER through dialogue ("As you remember, our magic works like this"). Only through action, objects in frame, consequence.

Strong technique: show the system in action → the hero reacts to it as ordinary → the audience understands that this is normal here.
