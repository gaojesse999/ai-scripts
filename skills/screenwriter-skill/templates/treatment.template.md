## TREATMENT-CHECK PRINCIPLES

After filling in — go scene by scene and answer:

1. **Does each scene begin with "because"?** (Sc. N happened, BECAUSE in sc. N–1, X happened.)
2. **Does each scene move a value** from + to – or back?
3. **Does each scene work on the project's controlling idea?**
4. **If you remove the scene, does the film change?** (If no — the scene is superfluous.)

If even one answer is "no" — the scene needs to be re-done or cut.

---

## AUDIT TAGS

When you find a problem — tag it:

- ⚠ **[CAUSALITY]** — scene doesn't follow from the previous
- ⚠ **[VALUE]** — no movement of value
- ⚠ **[BIBLE]** — conflict with the character bible or world rules
- ⚠ **[PACE]** — too fast / too slow for its function
- ⚠ **[INFO DUMP]** — exposition delivered through dialogue
- ⚠ **[REPEAT]** — repeats something that was already there
